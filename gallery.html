Exercise Demonstrations

Running is a popular aerobic exercise that improves
cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and boosts mood and overall well-being by moving rapidly on foot.

Cycling is a low-impact cardiovascular exercise that enhances cardiovascular fitness, strengthens leg muscles, burns calories, and reduces joint stress

Weight loss exercise involves physical activities that burn calories, increase metabolism, and promote fat loss, targeting various muscle groups and energy systems in the body.

HIIT cardio exercises, such as sprinting, jumping jacks, and burpees, can be combined with rest periods for a progressive workout

A warm-up is essential before any workout. It helps prepare your body for more strenuous activity and reduces the risk of injury
